About Big T's

Welcome to Big T's Chophouse, where tradition, bold flavors, and great entertainment come together for the ultimate dining experience! At the heart of our kitchen is Big T, a master chef renowned for his expertly crafted classic steaks. With years of experience and a deep passion for grilling the finest cuts, Big T has perfected the art of delivering tender, flavourful steaks that keep our guests coming back for more.

At Big T's, we offer more than just incredible food. Our restaurant is also the place to be for live music events, creating the perfect atmosphere for a relaxed and fun evening out. Whether you're enjoying the soulful sounds of local country bands, the energetic buzz of local rock bands or the twang of an acoustic set, you'll experience the perfect pairing of great food and lively entertainment.

Join us at Big T's Chophouse for a meal that satisfies your cravings and country tunes that lift your spirits. From steaks to sides, and from great food to great music, we offer an experience like no other.
